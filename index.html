HOW WE
STARTED BAKING?

The owner of the cafe and the author of all the desserts on the menu is Anna Nikulina:
Almost several years ago I came from Moscow to Almaty. Then my activity was connected with catering, but opening my own establishment was not in the plans. Having arrived here, I had a brilliant opportunity to start everything from scratch and devote more time to my favorite hobby - making desserts.
At first, I prepared desserts to order with delivery and the business began to grow actively. We got a workshop - literally a month later. We worked in this format for a year and a half, developing the project through Instagram. Our customer base grew, strengthened, and we grew to the point that we opened a small pastry shop with desserts to go.
